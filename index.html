I am a Creative.

I believe that every person is gifted with creativity - each to their own disposition, each honing their talent in pursuit of their magnum opus. In this pursuit, always remember to take stops and that it is okay to be stuck. As said by Sylvia Plath from 'The Unabridged Journals of Sylvia Path', "The worst enemy to creativity is self-doubt."

This piece was created because of my love for music - as to my other pieces - and because I personally love the band The 1975.

TOOTIMETOOTIME is a song about unfaithful relationships. The design is inspired by minimalism and modernism.

The song used is Next Level by the K-Pop girlgroup Aespa. The sound is very experimental and thus the design incorporated into the motion design was as well experimental and fun. A lot of different styles are combined.
